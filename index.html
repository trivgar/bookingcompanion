How it works

Share your link

Your booking page lives at yourname.bookingcompanion.app. Put it in your Instagram bio, text replies, business card. One link, always works.

They pick a time

Clients see your services, prices, and available slots based on your real schedule. No account needed, no app to download.

You approve

Get a text the moment a request comes in. Tap to confirm or reschedule. Nothing auto-books without you.

They get notified

Your client gets an SMS and email confirmation with appointment details and a link to reschedule or cancel if plans change.

Everything you need

SMS Notifications

Booking alerts to you, confirmation texts to clients, and automated reminders before appointments.

Google Calendar Sync

Confirmed appointments sync to your Google Calendar automatically. Your schedule stays in one place.

Custom Themes

Pick an accent color and choose dark or light mode. Your booking page matches your brand, not ours.

Client Management

Clients are saved automatically from bookings. View history, contact info, and notes in one place.

Business Hours

Set your hours for each day of the week with multiple time ranges. Clients only see times you're actually available.

Personal Events

Block off lunch, errands, or days off. Personal events hide those times from your booking page automatically.

Square Import

Switching from Square? Upload your appointment CSV and we'll import your clients, services, and full history automatically.

Insights

See revenue trends, busiest days, top services, and client retention at a glance. Know how your business is doing.

Built for you, not against you

No forced auto-booking. No client accounts or app downloads required. No percentage taken from your services. You set your prices, you control your schedule, you approve every appointment.

The client-facing booking page is entirely optional — turn it on when you're ready. Booking Companion works great as a standalone calendar, client manager, and appointment tracker even without it.
